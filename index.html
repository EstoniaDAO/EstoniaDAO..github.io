What is E-Residency?

Estonia is creating the first borderless digital jurisiction for global citizens. The e-Residency program enables foreigners to apply for a digital residency in Estonia, even though they don't actually live there. As an e-resident, you can create a fully legally compliant company within the EU, access banking facilities, pay taxes, securely sign documents - all completely online.

en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E-Residency_of_Estonia

What is a DAO?

DAO stands for "Decentralized Autonomous Organization" which is a smart digital organisation that can self-govern. In the case of Estonia DAO, it is made up and governed by token holders that are ID verified E-Residents with equal voting. Each verified member receives 1 membership token, called an EST token.
